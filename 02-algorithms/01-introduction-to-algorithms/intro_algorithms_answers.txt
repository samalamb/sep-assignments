Three Algorithms

1. Making Brewed Coffee
  - Plug in coffee maker
  - Choose the yield size
  - Fill tank with appropriate amount of water
  - Add in appropriate amount of coffee grounds
  - press the brew button
  - Once finished pour into cup

2. Making a Latte (using an espresso machine)
  - Designate cup size
  - Pour in milk to pitcher
  - Start steaming milk
  - Queue espresso shots to be pulled
  - Put cup so that it catches the shots
  - Wait for shots to finish pulling
  - Pour milk into cup
  - Serve

3. Making Toast
  - Get a slice of toast out of the bread bag
  - Put slice of toast into the toaster
  - Pull down on the spring
  - Wait for toast to be prepared
  - Serve on plate
